Folder 'models' should be inside you samp/omp-server, like scriptfiles, plugins or gamemode are.
This folder is to samp/omp-server recognize the models when loading.
Maybe to be generate you must cut and paste in your samp/omp-server.
try changing the "MODELS_PATH" in 'index.php' to your samp/omp-server directory, this should work and be automatic after.
